TaxCrackers

TaxCrackers is a liquid democracy app giving taxpayers voting rights to establish a fair tax distribution system using blockchain technology.

We want to empower individual Canadian taxpayers to have access to tax distribution projects and improve transparency in the process. We will work with government agencies like Service Canada by providing tools like the TaxCrackers key, so taxpayers will have better control over their tax dollars.
